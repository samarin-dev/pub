Quick start guide

  • Step 1: Prepare your device
  • Step 2: Connect (wired)
  • Step 2: Connect (Wireless)
  • After work with app (!!!)

To work with the application, the phone must be set to USB debugging mode, brief instructions are available on the start screen.

These are more detailed instructions

To perform this action, you need to unlock the developer menu first, to do this go to Settings -> Phone info and tap on "Build Version" more than >20 times (path may vary by device). If done correctly, you will see a pop-up message "You have become a developer!".

Now go to the Developer menu (it can be located in different paths, but usually it is Settings -> System -> Developer Options). In this menu, enable the "Debug by USB" option to work via wired connection or "Debug by WiFi" to work via wireless connection (note - the cable used to connect the device must support data transfer, in case of wireless connection the computer and smartphone must be in the same local network, ideally using WiFi dongles in distribution mode to avoid problems during work).

Connect your device with your PC using a cable, please ensure that: your cable supports data transfer, USB-debugging is on.
If previous steps done correctly you will see dialogue window on your device, press "Allow" to allow debugging and work with an app.

Note: highly not recommended for novice users

Connect to your PC hot-spot (recommend), or to your local Wi-Fi network (notice: PC and Android device must work in same network), please ensure that you: turn on Wi-Fi debugging.

If previous steps done correctly, you will see dialogue window, press 'Allow' to allow wireless debugging and work with an app.

Then, go to the app, open wireless connection window, enter pair code, host and port of your device (can be found at Developer menu -> Wi-Fi debugging) and press 'Pair' button.
Then, again go to the app, open wireless connection window, enter host and port of your device (can be found at Developer menu -> Wi-Fi debugging), remove 'Pair devices' checkbox and press 'Connect' button.

If previous steps done correctly you will see connected device in list.


Direct wireless connection (for devices with Android lower then 9.0)

Connect your device via wire (See: 'Step 2: Connection (wired)'), select your device in Apps screen.
Then, open wireless connection window, remove 'Pair devices' checkbox and press 'Connect' button.
Disconnect your device from PC (Do not turn off debugging and Wi-Fi), and open again wireless connection window, enter IP of your device (can be found at About Device -> Network) and port 5555, then press 'Connect' button

If previous steps done correctly you will see connected device in list.

Turn off debugging to protect your device from malware injection and enjoy of new experience :)

How to

Uninstall/disable/enable one or multiple applications?

Go to Apps screen, then select app(s) and select preferred method on the right side panel (Disable, enable or remove)

How to get *.apk of installed application?

Go to Apps screen, then select app and press download button, in dialogue window type or select destination path for your *.apk, then select *.apk from list and press 'Download'

Notice: Application may contain multiple *.apk`s, download of multiple *.apk`s (no matter - from one or multiple apps) does not supported yet (actual for 2024.05 version and lower) 

How to create uninstall script? (*.aaus)

You can create it from selected app(s) on Apps screen or use editor on Scripts screen

I have problems with my device after uninstalling/disabling apps, what i can do?

Notice: Uninstalling of application highly not recommended novice users

Firstly - go to the Apps screen, set filter disabled and try to enable this applications, after problem be solved you can try disable applications again one-by-one and check device for troubles in work

If you remove your apps you have only one solution - Factory reset, information about how to do it for your device you can found on internet

If this does not help - contact authorized service center of your device manufacturer, but please be patient: warranty support almost always does not be available for your device after performing these manipulations
